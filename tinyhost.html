Deploy to TinyHost

Follow these steps to deploy your HTML files to TinyHost:

  1. Step 1: Create a Zip File

    Compress all your project files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) into a single .zip file. Ensure that your main HTML file is named `index.html`.

  2. Step 2: Go to TinyHost

    Visit TinyHost and sign in or create a new account.

  3. Step 3: Upload Your Zip File

    Click on "New Website", then "Upload Zip File". Choose the .zip file you created and upload it.

  4. Step 4: Deploy Your Site

    Follow the prompts to complete the deployment. Once your site is deployed, TinyHost will provide you with a URL to access your site.

If you need further assistance, refer to the official TinyHost documentation.
